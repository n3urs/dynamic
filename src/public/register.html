Participation Statement

All climbing and bouldering activities have a risk of serious injury or death. Participants must be aware of and accept that even if they follow all good practice there may still be the risk of accident and injury. It is the responsibility of the participant to adhere to the conditions of use.

Conditions of Use

By signing this document, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by the rules and conditions set out by BoulderRyn when using the facilities. These include but are not limited to:

  • General Safety — Follow all posted rules and staff instructions at all times.
  • Bouldering — Climb only to the maximum permitted height. Check landing zones are clear before climbing.
  • Climbers Aged 8-17 — Must be supervised by a responsible adult at all times.
  • Warm Up and Training Area — Use equipment safely and within your ability.
  • Photography — Ask permission before photographing other climbers.
  • Matting Warning — The matting is designed to reduce impact but does NOT eliminate the risk of injury from falls.
  • Duty of Care — You have a duty of care to yourself and others using the facility.
