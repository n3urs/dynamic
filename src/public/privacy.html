Privacy Policy

Climbing Gym

1. Who we are

We are the data controller for the personal information you provide to us. We are committed to protecting your personal data and complying with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2. What data we collect

  • Identity data: First name, last name, date of birth, gender
  • Contact data: Email address, phone number, home address
  • Health data (special category): Medical conditions or injuries you disclose voluntarily for safety purposes
  • Emergency contact: Name and phone number of your emergency contact
  • Visit records: Check-in/out times and dates
  • Transaction records: Purchases, pass types, payments made
  • Waiver records: Signed liability waivers and consent forms
  • Profile photo: If you choose to upload one

3. Why we collect it and legal basis

PurposeLegal basis
Managing your membership and accessContract
Processing paymentsContract
Safety waivers and induction recordsLegal obligation & legitimate interest
Medical information (for safety)Explicit consent (Article 9 UK GDPR)
Emergency contact detailsLegitimate interest (safety)
Sending membership/booking emailsContract & legitimate interest

4. How we store and protect your data

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted in the European Economic Area (AWS EU-West region). All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Access to personal data is restricted to authorised staff only. We do not store payment card numbers — payments are processed by our card payment provider (Dojo/Paymentsense) who are PCI-DSS compliant.

5. How long we keep your data

  • Active members: For the duration of your membership plus 3 years
  • Waivers: 7 years (legal requirement)
  • Transaction records: 7 years (HMRC requirement)
  • Inactive accounts: Deleted after 3 years of inactivity, with prior notice

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access a copy of your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Erase your data (right to be forgotten, subject to legal obligations)
  • Restrict processing
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent for medical data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the gym directly or speak to a member of staff.

7. Third parties

We share your data only where necessary:

  • Dojo (Paymentsense): Card payment processing
  • GoCardless: Direct debit processing (if applicable)
  • AWS: Secure cloud infrastructure hosting

We do not sell your data to any third party.

8. Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
